Mega Blaziken

Blaziken can Mega Evolve into Mega Blaziken.

As Mega Blaziken, it loses its yellow markings and gains black markings on its upper legs and chest. Its hair-like feathers take on a wing-like shape and point upward, and the portion around its chest is rough around its shoulders.

The crest on its head changes into a flat horn that curves backward. The fire it creates on its wrists is now longer.
