Sylveon

Sylveon is a quadrupedal, mammalian Pokémon.

It has light blue eyes, long ears with blue interiors and thicker fur, a tiny nose, two stray tufts of fur on top of its head, and two pointed teeth which can be seen when it opens its mouth.

Sylveon is the only known Pokémon that can have Pixilate as an Ability without the use of Mega Evolution, and in Generation VIII and Pokémon Scarlet and Violet, it is the only Pokémon that can have Pixilate at all.
